**CamToCode** ([camtocode.com](https://camtocode.com)) lets you point your phone at code on a laptop screen and get **copy-ready source code** in seconds — with AI that understands indentation, brackets, and programming symbols.

## Why developers use camera OCR for code

Typing code from a screen is slow and error-prone. Generic OCR apps treat code like plain text and break indentation. CamToCode is built **for source code**:

- **AI Vision OCR** (Quick, Standard, Smart, Precision tiers)
- **Multi-frame capture** for sharper results
- **ROI crop** to scan only the code block
- **Cloud history** to save, edit, and share exports

## Quick start (free)

1. Open [camtocode.com/try](https://camtocode.com/try) for one free scan, or [sign in](https://camtocode.com/login) for the full free tier.
2. Point your phone camera at the code on your laptop.
3. Tap **Enlarge** and crop the region of interest.
4. Press **Start** → **Stop** (or use **Photo** for a single capture).
5. Copy or save to your [History](https://camtocode.com/history).

## Tips for best accuracy

- Use **Standard OCR** (recommended) for most laptop screens.
- Enable **Night Mode** if the screen has glare.
- Hold the phone steady; use **Auto Re-capture** for long files.
- Pair with free **[Scroll Automation](https://camtocode.com/scroll)** on your laptop — no install required.

## Long files without manual scrolling

Open [camtocode.com/scroll](https://camtocode.com/scroll) on your laptop, load your local file, and auto-scroll in sections while your phone captures each chunk. See our [docs](https://camtocode.com/docs) for the full workflow.
